Services of Thoppur Mani Caterings

We offer variety of catering services say Engagement Catering, Wedding catering, Baby shower catering, Baby naming catering, Ear piercing Catering, Birthday Catering, Housewarming catering, Corporate catering, Anniversary celebrations, Our door catering, and Private Party etc.,

Engagement catering services: This is an occasion where not only two hearts are engaged. Two families share their views, expectations on each other. We stand a step forward to fulfill their ideas about food arrangements along with our tasty food items, clean and clear servings. Many of the foodies are satiated with our yummy-tummy items and rewarded our presentation. What else we want?

Wedding catering services: South Indian marriages are really glorifying and conducted in a grandeur manner than the engagement. Now every couple plans their marriage, particularly foodstuff menus. We take this burden from you, plan & execute the work accordingly without any mess up. Our skilled catering crew will help you from planning the marriage menu, bringing out umpteen varieties, availing your people with gourmet foods. We cover from the seating to dessert serving. So, be free from your marriage catering plan, we are here to satiate all of you.

Corporate catering services: Our corporate catering experience is prevalent and handled many Corporate events. We intensively plan the occasion along with the company’s personnel head and understand the individual’s choice of appetite, deliver them hygienic food in a cordial atmosphere. Our tasty & nutritious foods always make the events memorable.

Birthday Catering services: Birthday especially for kids is a wonderful moment. The birthday baby along with kith and kin, others enjoy every minute and bless the child with warm wishes. We have certain birthday planners to make the celebration unforgettable. Our timely delivery along with perfection in every corner gives the customer double fold happiness.

Private Party: It is normally celebrated as a private occasion where close friends, relatives would be invited. We make the party as the day of pleasure, colors, with lip-smacking food menus.

Our door catering: Such type of catering is done for massive crowds. Our separate kitchen, special menus, a gathering of chief chefs, jaw-dropping taste, novel recipes take the entire crowd into the heaven of the food world. Every munch mesmerizes the mind, and every bite is beloved.
