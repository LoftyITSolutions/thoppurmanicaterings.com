About – Thoppur Mani Caterings

Thoppur Mani Caterings hail from Thoppur, Erode District, Tamilnadu have been serving for nearly 23 years in Food and Hospitality Industry successfully. We offer an unparalleled personalized service based on the need of the customers. Our caring towards pure, authentic ingredients, hygienic preparation, natural tasting agents and serving with pleasing manners escalated our values in the Hospitality Industry.

A team of experienced chefs, diversified menus, drooling taste, colorful and healthy food with an affordable cost are the greatest factors behind our abundant growth. We have a strong and focused team who can sumptuously cook from the least 10 members to a huge crowd of 3,00,000 individuals.

The food items never compromise on the taste, quality and utmost care will always be maintained. We are expert in South Indian, North Indian, Continental, Starters, Desserts and Chinese cuisine. Our familiar cooking area is fresh and organic based Vegetarian varieties and Ambur Mutton Biriyani, Thalapakkatu Biriyani, Hyderabad Biriyani, Tandoori items are a few of our special items in the Non-vegetarian menu. Customized and innovative foods fascinated many of our customers in their occasions. Such traits, made us in “Be wanted” status.

Mr. T.A. Manimaran, the Proprietor of “Thoppur Mani Caterings” is a simple, smart working and food-friendly human. He stepped into the catering field in the year of 11 and has been trained by many supremos. His eager towards learning the “Cooking and Serving Art” with dedication, perfection induced him to start up Thoppur Mani Caterings. Mr. Manimaran along with his skilled cooking crew catered to many orders, satisfied the customers and earned a renowned name across South India.

We strive to make your every event a complete success with joy and fun. Our commitments towards delivering consistent top quality service are our untold vision. We sincerely care and accommodate the needs of even a small event with perfection and dedication.
